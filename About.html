Reflection

Taking this class I was expecting a little more than what I came across at first, starting out with Notepad++ and learning the basics, coming from CSIS 1400 the previous semeseter it felt a little slow to start and a bit of a let down. My friends who had taken the class already helped to get out of the slump from the start offering a different coding software and upgrading the experience to get me on track. This led to a greater understanding behind it all as the coding became less troublesome and more streamlined allowing me to experiment and try to be more creative in my own way. Lacking some creativity myself though I was left disappointed with some of my projects until we were introuduced to Bootstrap which helped to improve the look and feel of the websites making everthing come together the way I could envision it. The joy I felt working on some projects kept me wanting to do more and trying out different things I found online and continued to help me expand my knowledge in Web Development. The class was great and my experience with some coding before helped to get me through and come out better than before.
